Enhance slide-show

The theme of energy represents the guideline of my study; ENHANCE is in fact a place where energy is understood as sustainable progress both on an environmental and human level. The space is therefore full of vitality and energy, declined in the stimulating images projected on the screens, in the tools for movement, in the interior design, in the minds and bodies of users, in the atmosphere created, in the energy drinks and in the exhibition hall. The result is an extremely fast-paced and functional environment.
