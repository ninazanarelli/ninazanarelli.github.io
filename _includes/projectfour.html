The College Community slide-show

The main focus of the residences for Alzheimer's patients is "Home Feeling". The main objective is to develop in patients a sense of belonging that can make them feel part of the place where they are, coming to perceive and define it as "home". This is done by designing a space tailored to the guests that meets their practical, functional and emotional needs, thus creating a Therapeutic Habitat in all respects.
