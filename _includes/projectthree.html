NooN has been designed as a product to be released in 2036 in California. It is no secret that California is a state with very high temperatures. Due to climate change the average temperatyres will keep on rising and become more and more dangerous. The state of California is actively trying to reduce climate change with laws and assessments. Our concept is not only taking advantage of the sun, but is also contributes to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
