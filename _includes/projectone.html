The College Community video

The College Community app is a mobile app made for colleges to build a stronger community and for students to socialise, collaborate and study better. We charge colleges an annual fee for the service and customize the apps to their college’s needs.

The College Community slide-show

I’m sure you’re thinking that our product is comparable to maybe Linkedin, Facebook or Campus Labs but let me tell you why we’re different. Unlike them, we have an app that can be customised to each school’s students and varying needs. We are also helping students to grow both professionally and personally by giving them the resources to overcome barriers that may hinder their growth. And most importantly, because we’re students ourselves, we know firsthand the problems that our fellow students are going through and we know how to solve these problems best. We truly believe that we are the best team to bring this idea to colleges and students across the country.
