The Community App video

The Community App is a mobile app made for colleges to build a stronger community and for students to socialise, collaborate and study better. We realised the app in September 2020 and we had more than 500 downloads in one month, obtaining a lot of good feedbacks from students. We develop a second and improved version of the app, following students' suggestions, during the fall semester 2020. In the meantime we participated in numerous competitions to finance our app and ensure its operation.
